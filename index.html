ABC University

The First Step to Legacy Learning

Culturally rooted ABC flashcards that celebrate heritage, language, and identity.

Representation matters from the start. ABC University introduces little learners to letters, ASL hand signs, and culturally rich images that reflect who they are.

Why Representation Matters

Fewer than 10% of children’s books published in the U.S. feature Black characters. ABC University fills that gap from the very beginning of learning.

Meet the Creator

Hi, I’m Zerin — a dad, HBCU graduate, and educator. My pride in who I am was shaped by my years at Hampton University and by a family legacy of HBCUs. That experience taught me the power of representation. Too often, our children don’t get that reflection early enough. That’s why I created ABC University — flashcards that pair letters, ASL hand signs, and images rooted in culture.
